How to Use FDOWN.net

To use FDOWN.net, follow these 4 steps to download any Facebook video in HD or MP4 format.

1

Copy the Facebook video URL

Open Facebook, find the video, click the three-dot menu on the post, and select "Copy link" to copy the Facebook video URL to the clipboard.

2

Paste the URL into FDOWN.net

Visit FDOWN.net and paste the copied Facebook video URL into the input field at the top of the page.

3

Click the Download button

Click the Download button. FDOWN.net uses URL Parsing to process the Facebook video URL and generate download links.

4

Select quality and save the video

Choose HD Video for high-definition or MP4 for standard quality. Right-click the download button and select "Save link as…" to save the Facebook video to the device's download folder.

Frequently Asked Questions

There are 7 common questions about using FDOWN.net as a Facebook video downloader. Each question is answered below.

Where are videos saved after being downloaded?

+

Downloaded videos are saved to the device's default download folder. On Windows and Mac desktop browsers, the save location is the "Downloads" folder. On Android, downloaded videos save to internal storage under the "Downloads" directory or the DCIM folder. On iOS, downloaded videos save to the Files app, and some browsers save directly to the Camera Roll. The exact path depends on the browser and operating system settings.

Why is the video playing instead of downloading?

+

The video plays instead of downloading because the browser opens the MP4 file inline instead of saving it. Right-click the download button or download link and select "Save link as…" or "Save video as…" to force the browser to save the file. Internet Download Manager (IDM) users can configure IDM to intercept the download link automatically.

Does FBDOWN work on Mobile devices? (Android / iOS)

+

Yes, FBDOWN works on mobile devices, including both Android and iOS. Open Chrome, Safari, or any mobile browser on the device, navigate to FDOWN.net, paste the Facebook video URL into the input field, and tap the Download button. The video saves to the Downloads folder on Android or to the Files app on iOS. No Mobile App installation is required.

Can I download Live Facebook videos?

+

Yes, Live Facebook videos can be downloaded after the broadcast has ended and Facebook has saved the recording to the profile or page. Live Facebook videos that are actively streaming in real time cannot be downloaded mid-broadcast. Once the stream ends and the video appears on the Facebook profile, paste the Facebook video URL into FDOWN.net to save it as an HD Video or MP4 file.

Video with no audio / only audio

+

A video with no audio or only audio downloads when Facebook stores the audio and video tracks as separate streams. Download the HD Video version instead of the standard MP4 version, as HD Video downloads merge both audio and video tracks into a single file. If the issue persists with the HD Video download, the original Facebook video may have been uploaded without an audio track.

Does FBDOWN store downloaded videos or keep a copy of videos?

+

No, FBDOWN does not store downloaded videos or keep a copy of any video. FDOWN.net processes only the Facebook video URL to generate a direct download link. No video data is saved on FDOWN.net servers. FDOWN.net's Privacy Policy confirms zero video data retention. Data Privacy is maintained by processing all requests server-side without logging video content.

Will there ever be a Firefox addon?

+

A Firefox Add-on for FBDOWN has not been officially announced. FDOWN.net's online Facebook video downloader works fully in the Firefox browser without any extension. A Chrome Extension is available for users who prefer direct browser integration. Firefox users can use FDOWN.net via the website to download Facebook videos without a Firefox Add-on.
