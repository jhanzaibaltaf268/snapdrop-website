Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about downloading Instagram videos.

Can I download private Instagram videos? ▼
No — SnapDrop only works with public profiles. Private accounts require you to be logged in, which we don't support.
How do I download an Instagram Reel? ▼
Open the Reel on Instagram, tap Share → Copy Link, then paste the URL into the field above.
Can I download Instagram Stories? ▼
Yes! Copy the story link and paste it in. Note: stories expire after 24 hours on Instagram.
Are there any watermarks on downloaded files? ▼
No watermarks at all. You get the original file exactly as uploaded.
Does it work on mobile? ▼
Yes, SnapDrop is fully mobile-optimized and works on iOS and Android browsers.
